Why HealthVault?

Keeping track of health information can be challenging – from misplacing doctor's notes to forgetting your latest blood test results. HealthVault solves these problems by serving as a comprehensive, eco‑friendly platform that connects your medical records, smart devices and hospitals in one place. Our goal is to empower you to make informed decisions about your wellbeing and take charge of your health journey.

With HealthVault you'll never lose important details again – the app collects heart rate, exercise, calories burned and medical records directly from your smartwatch or phone. It even provides helpful tips to keep you on track, making wellness simple and sustainable.

Core Benefits

Centralised Records

Maintain blood tests, diet charts, calories and other medical reports in one secure location.

Actionable Insights

Identify patterns like low exercise or high blood sugar and get personalised suggestions to prevent future illnesses.

Financial Planning

Visualise health‑related expenses and plan ahead for surgeries or check‑ups using interactive graphs.

Emergency Support

Find blood and organ donors instantly during emergencies to save lives.
