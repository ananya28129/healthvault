Health Tracking

Aggregate and monitor your heart rate, steps, calories and other health data directly from your smartwatch or phone. View daily statistics and track your progress over time.

Insights & Predictions

HealthVault analyses your records to spot patterns such as insufficient exercise or elevated blood sugar. It provides personalised recommendations to prevent diseases and alerts you to potential future illnesses based on your current habits.

Your data remains private and secure while enabling proactive healthcare decisions.

Expense Planning

Make budgeting simple by keeping track of all medical expenses including hospital bills, medicines and upcoming procedures. Interactive graphs help you visualise costs and prepare financially for future check‑ups.

Education & Updates

Stay informed with up‑to‑date information on diseases, symptoms and preventive measures. The platform serves as a knowledge hub, empowering you with reliable medical content vetted by professionals.

Donation & Emergency

In urgent situations, quickly locate nearby blood or organ donors through our integrated network. HealthVault streamlines communication with hospitals to make lifesaving connections when it matters most.
